About the Website

The PUP Lagoon Food Court offers a vibrant selection of delicious and budget-friendly meals to fuel your busy days at Polytechnic University of the Philippines. Whether you're craving Filipino favorites, refreshing snacks, or something in between, our diverse range of vendors caters to every taste and keeps your wallet happy.

This website is a student project designed to help you explore the delicious and affordable food options at PUP Lagoon Food Court. It was created in partial fulfillment of the requirements for a Design and Analysis of Algorithms course.

Our goal is to provide a user-friendly platform that leverages the power of algorithms to streamline your food selection experience at PUP Lagoon.

Algorithm

Knapsack Backtracking in Action:

Imagine a knapsack (a backpack) with limited space, representing your budget or dietary restrictions. The algorithm treats each food item as an object with a weight (price) and a value (satisfaction). The Knapsack Backtracking method explores all possible combinations of food items, aiming to fill your knapsack (maximize your satisfaction) while staying within your constraints (budget or restrictions).

Constraint Propagation for Optimization:

To further optimize the search process, we incorporate Constraint Propagation. This technique identifies and eliminates irrelevant food combinations early on, focusing the search on options that meet your needs. By filtering out unsuitable choices, constraint propagation ensures the algorithm finds the most fitting combinations quickly and efficiently.

The Result: A Streamlined Food Selection Experience

Thanks to the Knapsack Backtracking algorithm and Constraint Propagation, our website can efficiently present you with a diverse range of food options that align with your preferences and budget. So, browse our menu with confidence, knowing that powerful algorithms are working behind the scenes to deliver a delightful dining experience.
